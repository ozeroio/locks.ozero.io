Step 2: Finding The Guess Numbers

** Please Remember the dial only has numbers 0-39 and when you cross over from 39 it goes back to 0 and not 40.

Guess Number (0-11)

The guess numbers fall from 0 to 11, now you need to set the dial to 0, apply heavy tension (more than what you did for the sticky number) and turn the dial counter clockwise until it stops. You will need to find two numbers, one even and one odd.

When the dial stops and if you can turn it back and forth between two half numbers the number in the middle is your guess number. If your number falls on two exact numbers keep going.

The numbers in the middle are your guess numbers.
